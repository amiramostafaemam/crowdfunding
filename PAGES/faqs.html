Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Crowdfunding?
Crowdfunding is a method of financing projects by collecting small donations from a large number of people online.
How can I donate?
You can donate by choosing the campaign you want to support, then using the available payment methods on the website.
Can I start my own crowdfunding campaign?
Yes, you can create a new campaign on the platform by following the registration steps and uploading your project details.
Are there any fees on donations?
Fees depend on the platform's policy, and you can check them before making a donation.
How can I track the campaign I donated to?
You can follow campaign updates through your account on the website or via email notifications.
